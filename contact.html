Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I rent an item?

Renting an item is simple! Just browse our catalog, select the item you need, choose your rental dates, and complete the checkout process. You'll receive confirmation details via email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. Some locations may also accept cash payments.

What if I need to cancel my rental?

You can cancel your rental up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a small fee.

How do I list an item for rent?

Sign up as a lender, then click "List an Item" in your dashboard. Fill out the item details, upload photos, set your price and availability, and submit for approval.
